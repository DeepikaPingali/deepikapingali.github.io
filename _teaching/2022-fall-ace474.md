Guided student teams through a Consumer Price Index (CPI) project modeled on U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics methodology, advising them on constructing a custom price index to capture how college students' consumption baskets have shifted amid recent tariffs and rising inflation. Graded assignments and exams and held regular office hours to support student learning.
